About Us

Baryp-Kel Airlines is one of the fastest-growing airlines in Central Asia. Since our foundation, we have been committed to making air travel safe, affordable, and accessible for everyone. With modern aircraft and an experienced team, we are proud to connect people across countries and continents.

Our Achievements

• Operating successfully on the market for 12 years
• More than 15 million passengers transported worldwide
• Over 120 destinations across Europe, Asia, and the Middle East
• Recognized as one of the most reliable airlines in the region
• Fleet of 30+ modern aircraft, meeting international safety standards

Affordable Pricing

We believe that comfort and safety should not come at a high price. Our ticket prices start from $49 for regional flights and remain competitive for international routes. Seasonal discounts, loyalty programs, and family offers make our services even more accessible for everyone.

Our Founders

Daniyal — CEO

Daniyal is the visionary leader behind Baryp-Kel Airlines. His mission is to build a company that puts passengers first, while ensuring financial sustainability and expansion to new markets. Under his leadership, the company doubled its fleet within 5 years.

Nurgaly — CTO

Nurgaly is responsible for technology, innovation, and safety. Thanks to his expertise, the airline introduced smart booking systems, digital boarding passes, and predictive aircraft maintenance. His goal is to make every flight both efficient and safe.

Looking Ahead

By 2030, Baryp-Kel Airlines aims to expand its fleet to 60 aircraft, open new direct routes to North America, and become a top choice for travelers seeking affordable and safe flights.
